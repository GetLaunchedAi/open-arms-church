Worship Services


Join Us for Inspiring Worship & Fellowship


At Open Arms Church, we welcome you to join us for powerful worship services where we gather together to praise God, hear inspiring messages from God's Word, and build meaningful relationships with one another. Whether you're visiting for the first time or you've been part of our family for years, you'll find a warm, inclusive atmosphere where everyone is welcome.



Service Times

  1. Sunday Morning: 9:00 AM - 12:30 PM - Full worship service with children's ministry available
  2. Wednesday Evening: 7:00 PM - 8:30 PM - Mid-week worship and Bible study
  3. Special Services: We also host special services for holidays and special occasions

FAQ's

Worship Services FAQs

Answers to common questions about our worship services, what to expect, and how to get connected at Open Arms Church.

  • We have two regular service times: Sunday morning from 9:00 AM to 12:30 PM and Wednesday evening from 7:00 PM to 8:30 PM. Children's ministry is available during our Sunday service.

  • You can expect a warm, welcoming atmosphere where you'll enjoy inspiring worship, a practical Bible-based message, and the opportunity to connect with friendly people. Our church family is inclusive and welcoming to everyone, regardless of where you are on your faith journey.

  • There is no formal dress code at Open Arms Church. We welcome you to come as you are, whether that's casual or more formal attire. What matters most is your presence, not what you wear.

  • Yes, our interactive children's ministry is available during our Sunday morning service, providing age-appropriate activities and lessons for children. Children are also welcome to stay with their families during worship.

  • Open Arms Church is a Pentecostal church affiliated with the Assemblies of God. We welcome people from all backgrounds and denominations who are seeking to grow in their relationship with God.
