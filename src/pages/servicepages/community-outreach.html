Community Outreach Programs


Serving Livonia with Love & Compassion


At Open Arms Church, we believe in putting our faith into action by serving our community. Our outreach programs are designed to meet practical needs and share God's love with those around us. Through food pantries, school supply drives, and other initiatives, we strive to make a positive impact in Livonia and surrounding communities.



Our Outreach Programs

  1. Food Pantries: Providing essential groceries and meals to families in need
  2. School Supply Drives: Ensuring children have the supplies needed for academic success
  3. Community Support: Connecting with neighbors and offering assistance where needed

How We Serve

  • Annual school supply drives

  • Volunteer opportunities for congregation members
  • Partnerships with local organizations
  • Ongoing commitment to community care
